Brown Bear

There are 16 distinct subspecies of Ursus arctos living within temperate habitats.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Weight: 120 to 1800 pounds
  • Life Span: 25 to 35 years
  • Habitat: Northern temperate

Brown Bears roam the northern hemisphere, are omnivorous creatures eating what is available during their active period from spring through late fall and can eat up to 100 pounds of food a day when preparing for winter hibernation.
